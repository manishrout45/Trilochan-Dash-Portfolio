Biography

I am Trilochan Dash, born in 1988 in West Bengal. I am the son of Late Nirakar Dash and I come from a poor family. My childhood was full of struggles, but I never gave up on my dreams. With dedication and hard work, I continued my education and built my career step by step.

In 2014, I started the Utkal Computer Academy to provide modern education to children and youth. Today, it is still running and guiding many students. My faith in God and my wish to serve society inspired me to do more. This is how I created the Utkal Foundation Trust, through which I try to help people in need. Along with my social work, I also work in the Administrative Department of Nalanda Institute. Managing both work and service is not easy, but it gives me happiness.

Over time, I have received many awards for my work. I take them as blessings from God and encouragement to do better. My life journey from poverty to service has taught me the value of hard work, honesty, and kindness. I believe that if we have courage and patience, nothing is impossible. My mission is to keep serving people and spreading knowledge for a better society.

“Empowering people is the first step to transforming society.”
